Our story

We here at Bikeable believe that everyone should be able to experience the joy of riding a bycicle. That idea actually was the main reason why our founding fathers, twins Tim and Tom Trappers, started the company in 1911.

In the roaring 20's, Tom's eldest son Harley started organising our yearly bycicle races.

By the late 30's both Tim and Tom's sons joined the company. From left to right: Tim, Marcus, Lucas, Tom, Elias, Tobias and Mattias.

Because of covid19, unfortunately this year we had to change our yearly bycicle race into a teambuilding day, but next year it will bigger and more fun than ever, so don't miss it!
